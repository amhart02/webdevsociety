About Us

Mission Statement

The BYU-I Web Design and Development Society is dedicated to fostering a vibrant and collaborative community for students passionate about creating digital experiences. Our mission is to spark interest in web development by providing hands-on learning opportunities, promoting innovative design, and connecting members with real-world industry insights. Through workshops, projects, and mentorship, we empower students to develop their skills, explore creative solutions, and prepare for successful careers in web development. Together, we aim to inspire the next generation of web creators.

Our Values

  • Spark interest in web development
  • Connection
  • Innovative Design
